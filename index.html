About Us
A Brief Journey

Started in 2011, Smart Solutions has grown into a trusted statutory compliance consultancy with a core team of dedicated professionals. We bring deep expertise in EPF, ESIC, PT, and Labour Law compliance.

With a stronghold in consulting, payroll and compliance services, we have ventured into the HRMS arena, offering state-of-the-art software services. Our round-the-clock phone and mail support ensures you're never alone in compliance challenges.

We serve diverse industry segments with special emphasis on Factories and S&CE establishments, handling everything from multi-entity setups to government inspections and assessments.
